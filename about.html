About Us

We are a group of coffee lovers who also happen to be freelancers. After years of struggling to find places to work that met all of our requirements we decided that enough was enough. This website is designed to help others find the perfect place to work and drink great coffee.


The most important things for us when considering where to go are:

  • Most importantly of all; the quality of the coffee
  • Whether there is wifi, and how reliable it is
  • Availability and location of plug sockets
  • And of course, the deliciousness of the baked goods and pastries!
