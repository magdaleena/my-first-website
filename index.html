Best coffee shops in London


Best coffee shops for freelancers in London
Where can you be most productive?

You’ve finally kicked the office habit and have started working freelance - congrats! That said, you’re probably looking for that perfect spot where you can plug your laptop, get some reliable wifi and focus on your work while sipping on a delicious coffee. Here are some best spots in town:

Camber Coffee

££££ Cafe/Bakery in Soho

Are you in central or just love the heart of London so much? If you have a couple of hours to spare between meetings or simply love the location (and friendly prices) you should definitely visit this spot. This coffee basically exists to serve laptop crowds. There are plenty of tables and sofas but it is popular so make sure you come early to reserve a seat. Oh… and did we mention food? You can get anything from a salad to a chocolate foundant, in case you have more of a sweet tooth (but hey, we don’t judge!).
